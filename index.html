Perfect to give to your event or lounge a touch of unique artistry, South Central creates an array of cool and bluesy atmospheres while providing the listener with dynamic interpretations of the “Swing Era” hits made famous by the likes of Frank Sinatra and Duke Ellington amongst others, and the Broadway show tunes we all know and cherish by composers such as Cole Porter and the Gershwin Bros.

The group’s South American background is energetically reflected in their unique flavor when performing Latin and Brazilian rhythms, as well as our Argentinian Tango.

A more contemporary and “hip-per” repertoire is also part of South Central’s Jazz Entertainment. Names like Miles Davis, Dave Brubeck and Herbie Hancock are a few of the modern Jazz giants whose legacy is portrayed by the band’s eclectic and ever-growing song list.

So, if you are either a jazz fan, or just want to sit back and relax to the sound of America’s greatest musical legacy, look no further, South Central will make your worries swing away…you dig?
